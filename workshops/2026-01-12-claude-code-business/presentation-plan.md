## Global Style Guide

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Green (#22C55E) - brand accent, CTAs, highlights
- Secondary: Dark Gray (#1F2937) - headlines, primary text
- Tertiary: Medium Gray (#6B7280) - body text, secondary information
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) with subtle gray gradients
- Accent: Light Green (#DCFCE7) - highlight boxes, callouts

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Heebo Bold, 48-54pt, Dark Gray
- Subheadings: Heebo Medium, 28-32pt, Medium Gray
- Body Text: Heebo Regular, 20-24pt, Dark Gray
- Code/Terminal: Fira Code, 18pt, Dark background with green text
- All text aligned RIGHT for Hebrew RTL

**Recurring Elements:**
- Green gradient bar at top of each slide (left to right gradient from #22C55E to #16A34A)
- Aviz avatar watermark in bottom-left corner (subtle, 10% opacity)
- Slide number in bottom-right corner
- Terminal/CLI visual motif throughout

**Background:**
- Clean white with subtle geometric pattern (hexagons at 3% opacity)
- Represents tech/code without being distracting
